2. Replace the multi-network architecture with a hierarchical policy network
Instead of 18 separate NNs with manually-designed decision trees connecting them:
- Train a single hierarchical model with shared feature extraction and multiple output heads.
- Use attention mechanisms to let the model focus on relevant parts of the game state.
- This reduces the brittleness of manually routing between networks and allows cross-decision learning.
